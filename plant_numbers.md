## Multi-Season Notation

Use semicolon to indicate succession planting:
- `1;5` = Plant 1 (Tomato) in first season, then Plant 5 (Cabbage) in second season
- `21;29` = Plant 21 (Lentil) in spring, then Plant 29 (Wheat) in fall

## Empty Cells

Leave cell empty (blank or "0") for:
- Paths
- Buffer zones
- Future plantings
- Areas needing more space
