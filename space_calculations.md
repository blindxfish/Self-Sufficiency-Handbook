# Space Requirements Calculation

Based on Section 1 (seed amounts) and Section 5 (yields and spacing).

## Calculation Method

For each plant:
- **Legumes/Grains:** Annual need (g) / Yield per m² (g) = m² needed
- **Potatoes:** Based on spacing: 30cm × 75cm = 0.225 m² per plant, 15kg ≈ 60-100 seed potatoes = 15-25 m²
- **Vegetables:** Based on spacing and yield
- **Trees:** 4-16 m² per tree (2×2 to 4×4 cells) with buffers
- **Shrubs:** 1-4 m² per shrub (1×1 to 2×2 cells)
